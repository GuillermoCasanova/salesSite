SALES consists of two people sitting in an apartment bedroom, working on pop songs by moonlight. The collaboration between Florida natives, Lauren Morgan and Jordan Shih, has been pumping out earworms since their first single, “renee,” in 2013. The duo self-released their debut EP in 2014, and were named “Ones to Watch” by Hype Machine + BBC 6. They have headlined tours in the US, Canada and Europe with sold out shows in major cities such as LA, SF, SD and Brooklyn. Since the release of their debut LP on April 20th, 2016, they have reached over 4.5 million streams on SoundCloud and 15 million streams on Spotify.

They are self-managed and fully independent, with SALES’ collective partnership serving as their label (SALES MEDIA LLC). Between tennis matches, dead-end jobs, and Thai lunch specials, they have continued to release music that has gained the attention of publications such as Pigeons & Planes, Stereogum, Pitchfork and Gorilla Vs. Bear.

The duo heavily relies on experimentation within their process, whether it is switching lead guitarists, moving over to let someone else hold the mouse, recording vocals through Apple headphones (rather than the stellar SM7B), or inserting a 404 into a ballad. Each track humbly explores new territory with every song, as SALES moves to better understand and refine their “AS IS” approach to music.

SALES LP

The SALES LP was completely written, recorded, and produced in an un-treated bedroom studio. The album was created under the ideology that you should make the music you want to hear. The addition of the SALES LP to their tiny repertoire is only the beginning of an ever-expanding sound, and a small step in a new approach to music: empowered by the digital listening experience, and completely independent of a record label.

2016 FALL TOUR

PRESS

“The intersecting arpeggios and yearning “I’ll be on my best” assurances from singer/guitarist Lauren Morgan feel like a Beach House record mistakenly played at 45 rpm. Meanwhile, Shih punches out slightly off-kilter percussion patterns from bone-dry drum samples that would sound more at home on a forgotten ‘90s rap record. It’s an awkward synthesis, endearingly broken and blissed at the same time. There’s something human in how SALES show the seams.” - PITCHFORK on SALES LP

“When you hear the word simplicity these days, it’s usually in reference to clean, minimal electronic beats, but these guys are doing it with guitar work and a more organic, raw style. If the songwriting wasn’t excellent, it wouldn’t work-- but the songwriting is excellent.” - PIGEONS & PLANES 

“One of our surprise favorite releases of the year” - GVB on SALES EP

“The Orlando duo toes the line of folk, indie pop, lo-fi and minimalism and refuses to commit to one genre, which is part of SALES' intimate appeal.” -The Le Sigh 

“I feel like I’ve been waiting to hear SALES’ music all my life.” - In Love Not Limbo

Their sound has been described as:
